# YNX Terms of Use Draft

Status: draft  
Last updated: 2026-06-13  
Canonical language: English

## 1. Scope

These terms are a working draft for YNX public website, public APIs, public
testnet services, documentation, and related hosted infrastructure.

## 2. Testnet-Only Notice

YNX is currently a public-testnet environment unless explicitly stated
otherwise. Testnet assets, balances, bridge routes, AI settlement flows, and
other testnet outputs are for testing and evaluation only.

Users must not treat testnet assets as real-money balances, bank deposits,
stablecoins, investment products, or redeemable production assets.

## 3. No Custody

YNX is intended to operate as non-custodial infrastructure. Users are
responsible for their own keys, wallets, credentials, session approvals, and
onchain actions.

## 4. No Investment Promise

Nothing in YNX services, documentation, or public materials should be construed
as:

- investment advice;
- a promise of token appreciation;
- a guarantee of profit;
- a solicitation to purchase securities.

## 5. Availability And Service Changes

Public services may be modified, rate-limited, suspended, reset, or removed at
any time, especially in a testnet context. YNX does not guarantee uninterrupted
availability.

## 6. Acceptable Use

Users must not:

- attack, disrupt, overload, or abuse YNX infrastructure;
- use the services for unlawful activity;
- attempt unauthorized access;
- submit malicious payloads or abusive automated traffic;
- misrepresent testnet outputs as audited production guarantees.

## 7. Open Source And Third-Party Components

YNX may rely on open-source software, third-party RPCs, clouds, or external
networks. Those components may fail independently of YNX.

## 8. No Warranties

Services are provided on an "as is" and "as available" basis to the maximum
extent permitted by law.

## 9. Limitation Of Liability

To the maximum extent permitted by law, YNX and related operators should not be
liable for indirect, incidental, special, consequential, or speculative losses,
including losses arising from:

- wallet or key misuse;
- testnet resets;
- API downtime;
- bridge test failures;
- AI output errors;
- third-party network failures.

## 10. Compliance Reservation

YNX may suspend, restrict, or refuse access where necessary for security,
abuse-prevention, sanctions compliance, or legal review.

## 11. Contact

The final published version should include:

- legal entity name;
- contact email;
- security disclosure email;
- governing law and dispute terms after counsel review.
